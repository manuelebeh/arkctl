# Pattern checklist (humanizer-inspired)

Scan for clusters. Rewrite; do not delete meaning.

## Content

1. Significance inflation
2. Notability name-dropping without context
3. Superficial -ing analyses (symbolizing, showcasing, fostering…)
4. Promotional language
5. Vague attributions
6. Formulaic challenges / thriving sections

## Language

7. AI vocabulary pile-ups
8. Copula avoidance (serves as / boasts)
9. Negative parallelisms / tailing negations
10. Rule of three
11. Synonym cycling
12. False ranges
13. Subjectless / false-agency fragments

## Style

14. Em/en dashes
15. Boldface overuse
16. Inline-header list spam
17. Title Case headings
18. Decorative emoji
19. Persuasive authority tropes ("At its core…")
20. Signposting announcements
21. Fragmented headers restating the heading
22. Diff-anchored writing
23. Manufactured punchlines / staccato drama
24. Aphorism formulas
25. Conversational rhetorical openers

## Communication

26. Chatbot artifacts and closers
27. Cutoff / speculative gap-filling disclaimers

## Density

29. Filler phrases ("in order to" → "to")
30. Excessive hedging stacks
31. Generic conclusions
